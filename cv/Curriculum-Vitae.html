Research

My general research involves Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing (NLP). At SEU, I particularly focus on entity linking in web tables, that is the task of determining the identity of entities mentioned in text. Potential application includes Information Extraction, Information Retrieval, Content Analysis, Question Answering, Knowledge Base Population, etc.
